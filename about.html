ABOUT US

STUDENTS FROM THE UNIVERSITY OF MAKATI

An invention must make sense in the environment in which it is used, not the environment in which it was first conceived. We are a group of six students who aim to impact other students' thinking, creativity, and innovation through our website.


As first year students from the University of Makati, we want to show new innovations that will make you understand the purpose of technology in our everyday lives because a lot of generations have no idea about its past, present, and future. Aside from that, this website will also acknowledge and reflect the efforts of innovative creators.


We hope that this website will provide you with the information you need to avoid visiting numerous different websites. We will never harm you by making sure that our website contains no viruses. To summarize, this website may appear to be simple, but the information it contains will add color and life to how we perceive our evolving technology.
