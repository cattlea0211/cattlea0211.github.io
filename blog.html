Inventions are all around us. Without inventions, the efficiency of how we do everyday things will be slow. Inventions are what make human minds creative and innovative. Some inventions are used to help poor people or people who need it. Some are used to help people with disabilities, and some are used for medical purposes. which makes inventions all around us important, whether they are big or small, From cooking items and storing food to refrigerate them to simple clothes and things that emit light like lamps, Everything is important, and we use inventions around us all the time.
